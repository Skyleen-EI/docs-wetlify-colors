# Color Picker

Go in the [Color Picker](https://colors.wetlify.com/color-picker) tool.

The Color Picker tool allows you to choose your favorite color and see its different information.

## Choose your color

The Color Picker is composed of :

- a saturation and brightness zone
- a hue selection bar
- an alpha selection bar

Choose the right color by playing with the hue bar.

Then you can change the saturation and brightness.

Finally, you can adjust the alpha of the color for transparency.

You can also adjust your color with HEX, RGB and HSV formats.

You will find on the right a preview of your color, a save button for it and all the information that you can copy by clicking on the <ClipboardDocumentIcon style={{
  width: '1rem',
  height: '1rem',
  marginX: '0.25rem',
  verticalAlign: 'middle',
}} /> icon.

## Save your color (requires an account)

Click on the **Save** button, a modal will appear.

Choose your project and color name, then click **Save**.

You will find your color in your dashboard.
